Analyst Perspectives

The future growth story, particularly around AI ASICs, is where opinions diverge. This section contrasts the official company line with commentary from J.P. Morgan and Jefferies.

FY26 Enterprise ASIC Revenue Forecasts

A visual representation of the differing expectations for the crucial enterprise ASIC project. MediaTek has extended its timeline, while analysts have significantly lowered their near-term forecasts.

MediaTek's View

  • ASIC Timeline: Project is on schedule for late Q3'25 tape-out and Q3'26 production.
  • $1B Revenue Target: Now expected to take 2-3 years, a shift from the previous "next year" goal.
  • Confidence: Confident in technical execution, competitive position, and rapid progress on 2nm with TSMC.

J.P. Morgan's View (Neutral)

  • ASIC Delays: Believes delays are persistent and sees risk of the project being scaled down.
  • $1B Revenue Target: Tone from management is "more guarded." JPM estimates a more likely **$0.6B** in 2026.
  • Consensus: Expects street estimates for FY25 to fall 4-5% due to weak Q3 guidance.

Jefferies' View (Buy)

  • ASIC Delays: Acknowledges delays, pushing revenue recognition to late Q4'26.
  • $1B Revenue Target: Slashed their forecast significantly to **$0.3B - $0.5B** for 2026.
  • Long-Term Positive: Maintains Buy rating, positive on long-term partnerships with Google and Meta, and sets a price target of NT$1,650.

Future Outlook & Key Initiatives

Despite near-term challenges, MediaTek is heavily investing in next-generation technologies and strategic partnerships to drive future growth.

2-Nanometer Process

First 2nm chip tape-out is scheduled for **September 2025**, positioning MediaTek at the forefront of next-gen semiconductor technology for both edge and cloud AI.

Dimensity 9500 Launch

The next-generation flagship mobile SoC begins mass production in Q3, with more customer and model adoptions than its predecessor, aiming to extend market share gains.

NVIDIA Partnerships

The **GB10** chip (for DGX Spark) ramps in Q3 2025, and the **C-X1** automotive solution (for premium vehicles) will start generating revenue in 2026.
