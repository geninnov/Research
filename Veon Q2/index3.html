Overall Conclusion

The company is highly focused on enhancing its digital revenue and growing that sector, as signified by the impressive growth in digital revenue and management explicitly stating that it plans to become a "digital first company with a telecom licence". Management's confidence, backed by organic growth, and the strategic plans to provide greater value with Al services all positively reinforce this opinion. The company's plans to expand ride-hailing services also appear positive. It will be interesting to see if autonomous Robotaxis become part of these services, most likely through partnerships. The company's focus on capex intensity is also a positive indicator for a growth-focused company.
