Implications for the Memory Market

Area of Impact ↕ Implication for Memory Market Implication for SK Hynix Implication for Samsung
Market Environment Confirms a strong cyclical upswing with tight supply and firming prices across DRAM and NAND. Benefits from favorable market conditions, validating its early focus on HBM. Also benefits, but faces pressure to overcome recent execution challenges.
HBM Market Share Validates HBM as the primary value and margin driver in the memory industry. Faces a credible challenge from a rapidly executing Micron. Must maintain its technology lead in HBM4. Micron's success raises the stakes. Must execute flawlessly on its roadmap to regain share.
Capital Expenditures Signals a new wave of investment focused on advanced DRAM nodes, benefiting equipment suppliers. Must match or exceed Micron's capex intensity in HBM and advanced nodes. Will likely accelerate its own capex to close the gap in HBM and defend overall market share.
Technology Roadmap Competitive frontier has shifted to advanced packaging and customization of HBM base logic dies. Must continue to innovate on its MR-MUF packaging technology, a key enabler of its leadership. Plan to use 4nm FinFET for its HBM4 base die is a key strategic bet to leapfrog competitors.
