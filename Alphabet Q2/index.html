Strong Performance in Q2 2025

Alphabet reports robust growth across all segments, driven by Cloud momentum and AI innovation.

Performance Overview

Total Revenues

Reported $96.43 billion, a 14% increase year-over-year, beating consensus by 2.57%.

Operating Income

Rose 14% to $31.27 billion, slightly exceeding consensus by 0.51%.

Diluted EPS

Delivered $2.31, comfortably beating consensus by 5.96%.

Google Cloud Revenue

Showed robust growth, increasing 32% year-over-year to $13.62 billion, surpassing consensus by 3.65%.

YouTube Ads Revenue

Reached $9.796 billion, exceeding consensus by 2.25%.

Free Cash Flow (FCF)

Reported $5.30 billion, significantly below consensus due to higher capital expenditures.

Financial Outlook & Guidance

✓

Consolidated Revenue Growth: Strong Q2 momentum expected to continue into H2 2025.

✓

Capex Increase: FY25 guidance raised to $85 billion (from $75 billion) for server investments and accelerated data center construction, driven by cloud customer demand, especially for AI products.

✓

Tight Supply Environment: Demand for compute expected to outstrip current capacity into 2026.

✓

Depreciation Pressure: Significant increase in capex will lead to higher depreciation, expected to accelerate in Q3.

✓

Headcount Growth: Expected in key investment areas for 2025, with a sequential increase in Q3 due to new graduate hiring.

✓

Cloud Backlog: Increased 18% sequentially and 38% YoY to $106 billion, indicating robust future revenue streams.

Key Takeaways from Q&A and General Comments

AI Strategy & Adoption

  • ●

    AI deeply integrated across products, driving growth and engagement.

  • ●

    AI mode in Search (100M+ MAU in US/India) and AI Overviews (2M+ MAU globally) expanding user interaction.

  • ●

    Gemini app has 450M+ MAU, processing 980 trillion monthly tokens.

  • ●

    Google Lens and Circle to Search saw highest growth (70%), indicating strong GenZ user adoption.

Cloud Momentum & Investment

  • ●

    Strong Google Cloud growth driven by high customer demand for AI.

  • ●

    Significant increase in 2025 capex to $85 billion for servers and data centers.

  • ●

    Capacity expansion output visible towards the backend of the year.

Monetization & Search Evolution

  • ●

    Advertising business managed for user outcomes and advertiser ROI.

  • ●

    AI Overviews monetizing at similar rates to traditional search.

  • ●

    AI search scene similar to early days of Web, indicating new competition.

Agentic Capabilities & Partnerships

  • ●

    Significant progress in agentic capabilities, broader adoption anticipated for 2026.

  • ●

    Huge improvement in internal coding agent mentioned by Sundar Pichai.

  • ●

    More partnerships expected with OpenAI on Google Cloud.

Dual Search/Gemini App Strategy & Device Evolution

  • ●

    Two-surface approach: AI mode in Search for info, Gemini app for conversational/creative tasks.

  • ●

    AI to spur innovation in new hardware (e.g., glasses), but mobile phones central for 2-3 years.

YouTube Performance & Talent

  • ●

    YouTube shows strong advertising revenue growth, Shorts monetizing effectively.

  • ●

    Subscriptions increasingly important and growing part of the business.

  • ●

    Google well-positioned to attract and retain top AI talent.
